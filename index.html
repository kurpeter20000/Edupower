about edupower Youth Foundation

EduPower Youth Foundation is a non-profit organization in South Sudan that seeks to improve access to quality learning materials and empower the youth through entrepreneurship.

Mission is to empower the youth and foster a brighter future through enhanced access to quality education, job creation, and nurturing the underprivileged with entrepreneurial skills to create positive societal changes.

Our Vision is to be a global leader in fostering a vibrant and inclusive society where youth have seamless access to quality education, abundant job opportunities, and a thriving entrepreneurial ecosystem.

1. We conducted one entrepreneurial program in Juba, where we trained 25 students from 5 schools 2. We carried out a one-week information session program in Bor where 390 beneficiaries from 9 schools benefitted from the project 3. We have secured 25,000 book donations from Book For Africa and will build our library in Bor this year.
